I am a forest ecology and landscape modeling researcher whose work connects silviculture, ecosystem services, forest carbon, biodiversity, remote sensing, geospatial analysis, restoration, and climate-adaptive forest management. My research spans tropical forestry systems and Pacific Northwest forest landscapes.

🌱 Research Focus

🌲

Forest Ecology

Forest structure, regeneration, biodiversity, disturbance, and ecosystem function across tropical and temperate systems.

🗺️

Landscape Modeling

Simulation of forest ecosystem services, carbon, timber, biodiversity, and management scenarios across spatial and temporal scales.

🛰️

Remote Sensing

Geospatial analysis, high-resolution imagery, tree crown detection, and vegetation monitoring for environmental applications.

🌿

Silviculture

Seedling establishment, nursery management, restoration, and evidence-based forest management practices.
