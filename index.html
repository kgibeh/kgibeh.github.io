Kelechi G. Ibeh

PhD Researcher
Environmental and Natural Resource Sciences
School of the Environment, Washington State University

I am a forest ecology and landscape modeling researcher whose work examines silviculture, forest ecosystem services, forest carbon, biodiversity, remote sensing, geospatial analysis, restoration, and climate-adaptive forest management across tropical and temperate forest systems.
