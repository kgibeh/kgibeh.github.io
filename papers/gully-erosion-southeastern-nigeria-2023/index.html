The menace of widespread gully erosion in southeastern Nigeria: litho-anthropogenic history and management and policy issues

Authors: F. C. Okenmuo, K. G. Ibeh, and S. E. Obalum

Year: 2023

Publication: Valedictorian Festschrift in Honour of Professor Peter Chinedum Nnabude

Pages: 61-79

DOI: Not available

Research Areas

Gully erosion, southeastern Nigeria, land degradation, litho-anthropogenic history, environmental management, soil conservation, erosion control, and policy issues.

Plain-Language Summary

This work examines the widespread gully erosion problem in southeastern Nigeria, emphasizing geological, human-induced, management, and policy dimensions. It is relevant to land degradation research, environmental planning, erosion control, and soil conservation policy.
