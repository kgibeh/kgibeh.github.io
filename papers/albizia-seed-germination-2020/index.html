Effect of pawpaw latex, plantain stem juice and sulphuric acid on seed germination of Albizia lebbeck (L.) Benth

Authors: A. O. Akinyele and K. G. Ibeh

Year: 2020

Journal: Journal of Research in Forestry, Wildlife & Environment

Volume/Issue: 12(2)

Pages: 277-285

DOI: Not available

Publication record: AJOL article page

Research Areas

Albizia lebbeck, seed germination, seed dormancy, pawpaw latex, plantain stem juice, sulphuric acid, nursery management, and tropical forestry.

Plain-Language Summary

This study examines treatments for improving seed germination in Albizia lebbeck, including pawpaw latex, plantain stem juice, and sulphuric acid. The work is relevant to nursery production, dormancy breaking, tropical tree propagation, and restoration forestry.
