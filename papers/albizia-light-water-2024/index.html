Investigating Light and Water Optimization for Early Seedling Establishment in Albizia lebbeck (L.) Benth

Authors: K. G. Ibeh and A. O. Akinyele

Year: 2024

Journal: South-east European Forestry: SEEFOR

Volume/Issue: 15(2)

Pages: 151-159

DOI: 10.15177/seefor.24-14

Research Areas

Albizia lebbeck, seedling establishment, light optimization, water optimization, silviculture, tropical forestry, restoration ecology, and nursery management.

Plain-Language Summary

This study examines how light and water conditions influence early seedling establishment in Albizia lebbeck, a multipurpose tree species relevant to restoration, nursery production, and tropical silviculture.
