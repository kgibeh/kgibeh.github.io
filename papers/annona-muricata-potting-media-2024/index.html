Early Growth Response of Annona muricata (L.) Seedlings to Different Potting Media

Authors: K. G. Ibeh, E. L. Anozie, C. C. Osigwe, and P. C. Umeh

Year: 2024

Journal: Asian Journal of Research in Agriculture and Forestry

Volume/Issue: 10(1)

Pages: 135-144

DOI: 10.9734/AJRAF/2024/v10i1277

Research Areas

Annona muricata, potting media, seedling growth, nursery management, tropical forestry, forest regeneration, soil media, and plant establishment.

Plain-Language Summary

This study evaluates the early growth response of Annona muricata seedlings to different potting media. The work is relevant to nursery management, seedling production, tropical tree propagation, and restoration-oriented forest regeneration.
