Growth response of Dennettia tripetala (G. Baker) to different organic manure at the early stage

Authors: E. L. Anozie, K. G. Ibeh, N. B. Ndulue, A. L. Nwachukwu, and C. L. Umeh

Year: 2020

Journal: European Journal of Agriculture and Forestry Research

Volume/Issue: 8(3)

Pages: 17-26

DOI: Not available

Research Areas

Dennettia tripetala, organic manure, seedling growth, nursery management, tropical forestry, soil fertility, organic amendments, and forest regeneration.

Plain-Language Summary

This study evaluates how different organic manure treatments affect the early growth response of Dennettia tripetala. The work is relevant to nursery management, seedling production, organic soil amendments, and tropical forest restoration.
